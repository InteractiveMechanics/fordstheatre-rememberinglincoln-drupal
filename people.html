People

From politicians to poets, from soldiers to every day citizens, reaction to the assassination was very personal. Peoples' experiences during the war and how they felt about Lincoln's role as President created a very complex political and emotional landscape. These personal reflections represent the wide range of reactions experienced by citizens in 1865.
