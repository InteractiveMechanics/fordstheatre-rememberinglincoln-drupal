Exploring the life, legacy, and memory of Abraham Lincoln.

When President Lincoln was assassinated, public reaction varied from grief, to confusion, to celebration. 150 years later, how can the memories and reflections of everydat citizens help us understand what this event meant for the reunited nation and our future in the wake of the Civil War?
